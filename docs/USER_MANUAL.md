## 1. Public User
*Role: Anyone visiting the site without logging in.*

### 1.1 Browsing Articles
*   **Home Page**: View the latest issue and featured articles immediately upon landing.
*   **Archives**: Click **"Archives"** in the navigation bar to browse past volumes and issues.
*   **Viewing an Article**: Click on any article title to view its abstract, keywords, and author details. If the article is open access, you can download the full PDF.

### 1.2 Searching
*   Click the **Search** icon (magnifying glass) or navigating to the search page.
*   Enter keywords, titles, or author names.
*   Filter results by date or relevance.

### 1.3 Registration & Login
*   **Register**: Click **"Sign In"** -> **"Sign Up"** to create an account. You will need an account to submit articles or perform reviews.
*   **Login**: Use your email and password to access your dashboard.

## 2. Author
*Role: Researchers and academics submitting manuscripts.*

### 2.1 Dashboard Overview
Upon logging in, you are directed to the **Author Dashboard**.
*   **Active Submissions**: View manuscripts currently in the process.
*   **Action Required**: See submissions returned for revision.
*   **History**: View past accepted or rejected submissions.

### 2.2 Submitting a Manuscript
1.  Click the **"New Submission"** button on your dashboard.
2.  **Step 1: Details**: Enter the Title, Abstract, and Keywords.
3.  **Step 2: Upload**: Upload your manuscript file (PDF format required).
4.  **Step 3: Confirm**: Review your details and click **"Submit"**.
5.  You will receive an email confirmation.

### 2.3 Tracking Status
Your dashboard table shows the current status of each submission:
*   `SUBMITTED`: Awaiting initial check by the editor.
*   `UNDER_REVIEW`: Sent to peer reviewers.
*   `REVISION_REQUESTED`: Reviewers have requested changes.
*   `ACCEPTED`: Scheduled for publication.
*   `REJECTED`: The submission was not accepted.

### 2.4 Submitting a Revision
If your status is `REVISION_REQUESTED`:
1.  Click on the submission title.
2.  View the reviewer feedback and editor comments.
3.  Click **"Submit Revision"**.
4.  Upload the updated PDF file addressing the comments.

## 3. Reviewer
*Role: Subject matter experts invited to peer-review submissions.*

### 3.1 Dashboard Overview
*   **Pending Reviews**: Number of manuscripts waiting for your feedback.
*   **Completed Reviews**: History of your past contributions.
*   **Assigned Manuscripts Table**: A list of papers currently assigned to you.

### 3.2 Performing a Review
1.  **Access**: Click on the title of an assigned manuscript in your dashboard.
2.  **Read**: Download and read the manuscript PDF (Note: Author details may be hidden for blind review).
3.  **Evaluate**:
    *   **Score**: Rate the paper (e.g., 1-10).
    *   **Recommendation**: Choose `Accept`, `Reject`, or `Revision`.
    *   **Feedback**: Write detailed constructive comments for the author and confidential comments for the editor.
4.  **Submit**: Click **"Submit Review"**. This action is final.

## 4. Editor-in-Chief / Secretary
*Role: Managing the publication workflow. (System Role: EDITOR or ADMIN)*

### 4.1 Editorial Dashboard
*   **Overview**: View stats on Total Submissions, Pending Vetting, and Active Reviews.
*   **Submission Management**: A master table of all submissions in the system.

### 4.2 Workflow Steps
#### A. Initial Vetting (Secretary)
1.  New submissions appear with status `SUBMITTED`.
2.  Click on the submission to review basic compliance (formatting, scope).
3.  **Action**:
    *   **Approve**: Changes status to `UNDER_REVIEW`.
    *   **Reject**: Immediately rejects the paper.

#### B. Assigning Reviewers
1.  Open a submission with status `UNDER_REVIEW`.
2.  Scroll to the **"Reviewers"** section.
3.  Select a reviewer from the dropdown list.
4.  Click **"Assign"**. The reviewer will receive an email notification.

#### C. Making a Decision
1.  Once reviews are in, the Editor reviews the feedback.
2.  **Action**:
    *   **Accept**: Moves to publication queue.
    *   **Revision**: Sends feedback to author for updates.
    *   **Reject**: Ends the process.

#### D. Publishing an Issue
1.  Go to the **"Issues"** tab (or "Publishing" section).
2.  Click **"Create New Issue"** (Volume/Number/Year).
3.  Select `ACCEPTED` articles to include in this issue.
4.  Click **"Publish Issue"** to make it live on the public site.
