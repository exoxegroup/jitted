Implementation Plan/Duration per Quarterly Publication
The following table outlines the structural steps and timing of activities for each quarterly issue of the Journal of Issues in Technical Teacher Education (JITTED).

S/N,Steps,Activities,Duration
1,Call for Publication,Verification of submission of articles by the publication committee.,4 Weeks
2,Submission for Peer Review,Sorting of articles for peer review/vetting based on area of specialization.,1 Week
3,Review Collection,Collection of articles from reviewers after peer review/vetting and committee meeting.,1 Week
4,Author Corrections,Sending reviewed articles to authors and verification of the corrected versions.,1 Week
5,Editing & Formatting,Editing of articles and verification of the Dummy Copy by the Editor-in-chief.,1 Week
6,Production Phase,Submission of Dummy Copy to the publisher and preparation for distribution.,2 Weeks
7,Distribution,"Distribution to authors, College library, National Library, and TETFUND Headquarters.",2 Weeks
,TOTAL DURATION,,12 Weeks
