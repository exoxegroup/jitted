Foreword
This volume focuses on Revitalizing Education for Sustainable Development amidst Security, Economy, and Health Challenges in Nigerian Education. It explores critical intersections of skill acquisition, ICT, Agricultural Education, and innovative teaching methods in Science and Technology.

"I recommend this volume to students, researchers, and staff for its invaluable contributions towards the development of Technology Teacher Education globally."

— Dr. Mursali Mohammed Jaji, Editor-in-Chief
